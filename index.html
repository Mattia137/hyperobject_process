HYPEROBJECT DESIGN PROCESS

This diagram serves as the project's spatial database and central nervous system. Rather than using a traditional folder hierarchy, every digital artifact (models, scripts, research) is tethered directly to its architectural node.

The workflow tracks the design's evolution across four converging phases:

Research: The Brief branches into parallel tracks of Site analysis, Media context, and Geological (Stone/Pattern) research.
Synthesis: Raw data crystallizes into spatial logic and footprint (Program, Massing).
Execution: Abstract concepts fuse into tangible systems (Skin, Structure, UE Environments) and the master Project Model.
Output: The master model generates technical documentation, animations, and final Open Source data.
